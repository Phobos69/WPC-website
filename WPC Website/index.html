NGONYE FALLS HYDROELECTRIC POWER PROJECT

Western Power Company is a Zambian Power developer building the Ngonye run-of-river hydraulic power scheme on the Zambezi river in the western province of Zambia, (the project)

The Ngonye Falls project will generate 180MW of clean, renewable energy for the people of Zambia and the Western Province.

The project is being developed and will be operated to the highest standards of environmental and social responsibility by a team of Zambian and international specialists.
