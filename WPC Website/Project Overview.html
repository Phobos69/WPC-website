Project Overview

Project Outline

Feasibility Study draft delivered in May 2018:

  • Low head, high flow, run-of-river hydroelectric power generation.
  • 180 MW installed capacity generating 855 GWh p.a. net for a 52% plant factor.
  • Adjustable weirs and embankments 3m high for head-pond control at 990 masl and to pass required environmental and social flow requirements - EFlows.
  • Barrage across the left (east) river channel with radial gates for sediment scouring, intake control and flood passing.
  • Trapezoidal canal 3 km in length from intakes to forebay.
  • Large forebay for load rejection and for control of turbines.
  • Pit powerhouse with 4 bulb turbines generating > 45 MW of output each.
