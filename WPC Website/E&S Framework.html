E&S Framework

  • ESIA being undertaken in accordance with:
    • Zambian Legal Requirements: Environmental Management Act (No.12 2011), EIA Regulations (SI No. 28 1997) and Environmental Management Regulations (SI No. 112 2013).
    • IFC Performance Standards (2012) and WB EHS Guidelines (2007).
    • IFC Good Practice Notes for Hydropower.
  • The Project will undergo an ESG assessment by an IHA Accredited Assessor.
  • The ESIA will be completed Q3 2018.
