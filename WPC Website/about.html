ABOUT

Western Power Company (WPC) is developing the Ngonye run-of-river hydroelectric power scheme on the Zambezi river in the Western Province of Zambia, (the “Project”).

The Project is being developed by WPC as an IPP under an Implementation Agreement with the Zambian Government and in partnership with the community of the Western Province of Zambia.

The Project is targeted to achieve Financial Close in Q4 2019. The major milestones remaining in the Project development period are: the EPC tender process and contract negotiation; approval by ZEMA of the Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (“ESIA”); agreement of a full Power Purchase Agreement (“PPA”) based on the long-form heads of terms agreed with ZESCO; and the debt financing process.

WPC is headquartered in Lusaka and managed by a team of experienced Zambian and international professionals with expertise in engineering, infrastructure development, finance and construction.
